Introduction#

Feel free to work in groups or individually on this. For each of the three questions below, the idea is to figure out what the code is doing and explain it. Upload the notebook with answers to the ~/shared_files/your_initials folder on a301hub

The notebook, groups all the satellite scenes by year and season, then goes over all the grouped dataframes, finding the scene in each season with the smallest cloud fraction, and builds a new dataframe with just those scenes.
